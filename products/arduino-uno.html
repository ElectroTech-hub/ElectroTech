Arduino Uno R3

₹1499.99

Specifications

Microcontroller ATmega328P
Digital I/O Pins 14
Analog Input Pins 6

Description

The Arduino Uno R3 is a microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P. It has 14 digital input/output pins, 6 analog inputs, a 16 MHz ceramic resonator, a USB connection, a power jack, and an ICSP header.
